Product Information

Creative elevates athletic performance during high intensity training by increasing the production of ATP. With multi-faceted actions, some of the benefits include promoting lean muscle growth and accelerated recovery.

Taurine Taurine regulates electrolyte balance, reinforcing muscle strength and stamina. It hones mental focus and promotes aerobic capacity.

Glycine Glycine bolsters protein synthesis. It also complements creatine activity by acting as a glycogen reservoir.This result makes glucose more readily available for energy release and perfusion.

Beta-Aline amplifies muscle function, reducing fatigue and supporting endurance. These processes accelerate the development of muscle mass.

Alpha Lepoic Acid the “power station” of cells, encourages nutrient partitioning as well as glycogen and amino acid availability. ALA’s anti-oxidant properties neutralize free radicals, preventing oxidative damage.

Magnesium participates in ATP energy synthesis to improve muscle growth and strength. It plays a vital role in muscle function, enhances oxygen uptake and stimulates energy production.
